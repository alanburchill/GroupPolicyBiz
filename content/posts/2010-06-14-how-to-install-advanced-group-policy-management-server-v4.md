### Installing the AGPM Server

The Advanced Group Policy Management Server is the central server that keeps track and makes changes to all the Group Policy Objects in the forest. **Step 1.** Start the Advanced Group Policy Management and select the "“ Server install." option. **Step 2.** Click **Next** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb112.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image117.png>) **Step 3.** Tick **I accept license terms** and then click **Next** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb113.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image118.png>) **Step 4.** Confirm the Application path and click **Next** **** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb114.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image119.png>) **Step 6.** Confirm the **Archive Path** and click **Next** **** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb115.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image120.png>) **Step 7.** Enter the AGPM Service Account details. This account needs to have full access to all GPO that you want to manage using AGPM then click **Next** **** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb116.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image121.png>) **Step 8.** Enter the Archive Owner account (e.g. Contoso\Alan ) this account is the first Full Control administrator in AGPM that is used to delegate permission to other users then click **Next** **** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb117.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image122.png>) **Step 9.** Confirm the Port (this needs to be the same as step 5 in the Install Client stage) and click **Next** **** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb118.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image123.png>) **Step 10.** Leave all the languages selected and click **Next** **** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb119.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image124.png>) **Step 11.** Click **Install** **** [![image](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image_thumb120.png)](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/image125.png>) **Step 11a.** Optional "“ Click on the Details button to see the components that will be installed.
