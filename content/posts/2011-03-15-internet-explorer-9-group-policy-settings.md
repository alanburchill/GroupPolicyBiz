### Updated: Internet Explorer 9 Group Policy Preferences

Currently there is no native (supported) way to use Group Policy Preferences with IE9 however you can you can easily work around this restriction. See my post at [How to enable Group Policy Preferences support for IE9](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/2011/03/how-to-enable-group-policy-preferences-support-for-ie9/>) . For a complete listing of all the IE9 Group Policy setting, where they can be found and how you install them to Active Directory go to [TechNet: Group Policy Settings](<http://technet.microsoft.com/en-au/library/ff986259.aspx> "http://technet.microsoft.com/en-au/library/ff986259.aspx")
