![](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/10/image20.png)A [new post](<http://blogs.technet.com/b/askds/archive/2010/11/01/new-directory-services-content-10-24-10-30.aspx>) on the [Ask the Directory Service Team](<http://blogs.technet.com/b/askds/>) blog has just announced a new hotfix that addresses an Access Denied issue when using Group Policy Preferences to schedule a task.

[KB2447414](<http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2447414>) \- User GPP Scheduled Task item fails to apply and logs event id: 4098 with 0x80070005 "Access is denied."
