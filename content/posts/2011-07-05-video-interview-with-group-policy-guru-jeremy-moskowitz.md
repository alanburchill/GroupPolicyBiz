In this video, fellow Group Policy MVP [Jermey Moskowitz](<https://twitter.com/#!/jeremymoskowitz>) recorded video interview at TechEd Atlanta. In this video he talks about what to expect with Group Policy in 2011 and he talks about his up coming release of PolicyPak v3.

> Jeremy also talked about PolicyPak 3.0, the new update being released to PolicyPak, and what the benefits of the software are for busy administrators.

PolicyPak its a great third party add on to Group Policy that enabled to lock down and configured almost any third-party application such as FireFox, WinZip Adobe Reader via Group Policy. For more information check out his web site at <http://www.policypak.com>
