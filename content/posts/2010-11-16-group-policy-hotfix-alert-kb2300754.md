![](https://www.grouppolicy.biz/wp-content/uploads/2010/10/image20.png)Microsoft have just released a new Group Policy related hotfix that resolves a memory leak with Group Policy Preferences in Windows Server 2008.

[KB2300745](<http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2300745>) \- A memory leak occurs in Svchost.exe when you apply or update Group Policy preference settings in Windows Server 2008
