If you have ever configured you Internet Explorer setting via the "Internet Explorer Maintenance" group policy setting you might be wondering however to remove these setting now you found [a few easier ways](<https://www.grouppolicy.biz/tag/internet-explorer/>) to do the same thing. Well its not all that obvious but if you go to User Configuration > Policies > Windows Settings you can then right click on "Internet Explorer Maintenance" and click "Reset Browser Settings" you are done"...
